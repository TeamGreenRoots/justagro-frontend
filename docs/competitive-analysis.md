# Competitive Analysis – JustAgro

## Market Context

The agricultural trade ecosystem in Africa operates within a highly fragmented and informal structure, where over 80% of transactions occur without standardized systems for verification, tracking, or accountability.

JustAgro is positioned not as a marketplace or financing platform, but as a **transaction infrastructure layer** that ensures every produce delivery is recorded, verified, and tied to a confirmed payment.

---

## Competitive Landscape

We identified three primary categories of competitors:

---

### 1. Agri-Fintech Platforms

**Examples:** ThriveAgric, Crop2Cash, Farmcrowdy

#### Strengths

* Established trust within rural communities
* Strong distribution via field agents
* End-to-end ecosystem support

#### Limitations

* Limited visibility into last-mile transaction execution
* No standardized system for delivery-to-payment verification
* Continued reliance on informal coordination channels

#### Insight

These platforms enable transactions but do not provide **verifiable proof of those transactions**.

---

### 2. Fintech Payment Infrastructure

**Examples:** Paystack, Flutterwave, Moniepoint, PalmPay

#### Strengths

* High transaction reliability and scale
* Strong payment infrastructure
* Widespread adoption

#### Limitations

* No agricultural context
* Payments not linked to delivery events
* No transaction-level proof tied to goods

#### Insight

They move money but do not capture **transaction meaning or validity**.

---

### 3. Agricultural Supply Chain Infrastructure

**Examples:** AFEX, Agrospace, AfrikFair

#### Strengths

* Deep value chain integration
* Strong institutional relationships
* Large-scale trade infrastructure

#### Limitations

* Not optimized for small, frequent transactions
* Limited real-time transaction verification
* Focus on macro trade efficiency

#### Insight

They optimize supply chains but not **transaction-level trust**.

---

## Primary Competition: Informal Systems

Includes:

* Word-of-mouth agreements
* WhatsApp coordination
* Paper records
* Cash transactions

#### Strengths

* Zero cost
* Familiar
* Easy to use

#### Limitations

* No accountability
* High fraud risk
* No verifiable proof

#### Insight

We must outperform informal systems in **simplicity and trust**.

---

## Market Gap

There is no system that provides:

> Real-time, verifiable proof of agricultural transactions at the point of delivery.

---

## Strategic Positioning

JustAgro acts as:

> The transaction and verification layer for agricultural trade

We:

* Capture delivery events
* Trigger and confirm payments
* Generate receipts
* Build transaction history
